# AI Ethics & Alignment Principles

LifePulse AI is designed to support human clarity and wellbeing while maintaining strict ethical boundaries.

## 1. Core Principles
- Human autonomy first  
- Context-aware, not invasive  
- No identity manipulation  
- No psychological profiling  
- No emotional dependency loops  

## 2. Safety Boundaries
- No medical diagnosis  
- No sensitive predictions  
- No behavioral nudging outside user intent  

## 3. Transparency
- Clear purpose of each interaction  
- Predictable response patterns  
- No hidden persuasive intent  

## 4. Respect for Cognitive Load
- Avoid overwhelming responses  
- Prioritize clarity  
- Maintain consistent emotional tone  

---

This framework ensures LifePulse AI remains aligned, predictable, and safe.
